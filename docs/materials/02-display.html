Exercise 1

Discuss the following graphic.

  • What is the main message?
  • Does the chart type work?
  • Is there a better chart type?
  • Draw an alternative version(s) using different chart type(s).

Exercise 1

Market share of visits to social network sites in November 2017,
found in the blog post on “Why you shouldn’t use pie charts” by The University of Melbourne

Exercise 2

Imagine you could get your hands on additional data on market shares of visits to social network sites.

  • What kind of data would you love to explore?
  • What would be stories you’d like to tell?
  • What could be suitable chart types to tell such a story?
